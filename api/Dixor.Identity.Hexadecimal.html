Remarks

Hexadecimal is the primary entry point for generating hexadecimal encoded identifiers in the Dixor.Identity library.

Hexadecimal identifiers consist of the numeric characters (0-9) and alphabetic characters (A-F or a-f), with configurable uppercase or lowercase output. Each generated byte is represented by two hexadecimal characters.

All identifiers are generated using a cryptographically secure random number generator to ensure high entropy and uniqueness.
