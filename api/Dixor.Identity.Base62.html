Remarks

Base62 is the primary entry point for generating Base62 encoded identifiers in the Dixor.Identity library.

Base62 identifiers consist of the numeric characters (0-9), uppercase alphabetic characters (A-Z) and lowercase alphabetic characters (a-z), providing compact, URL-safe identifiers with a higher encoding density than Base36.

All identifiers are generated using a cryptographically secure random number generator to ensure high entropy and uniqueness.
